# Model App Screenshot Organization

Screenshots are grouped by sequential page or flow to support page-by-page replication. Filenames keep the original `IMG_####` capture id so the raw sequence is still traceable.

Use `_contact_sheet.png` for a quick visual overview of the original capture order.

## Groups

### 01_home

Home tab states.

- `01_IMG_2618_home_empty_start.PNG` - Empty/first-start home state with suggested protocols.
- `02_IMG_2619_home_configured_stack.PNG` - Home with active protocol stack and compliance cards.
- `03_IMG_2649_home_scheduled_doses_due_today.PNG` - Home with scheduled dose cards for active protocols.

### 02_main_tabs

Top-level app tabs and main app surfaces.

- `01_IMG_2620_tracker_protocol_score.PNG` - Tracker tab with protocol score and medications.
- `02_IMG_2621_pulse_dashboard.PNG` - Pulse dashboard with highlights and cards.
- `03_IMG_2622_pulse_insights_chat.PNG` - Pulse insights/detail/chat-style screen.
- `04_IMG_2623_profile.PNG` - Profile tab with personal info, achievements, subscription, settings.

### 03_protocol_stacks

Optimization stacks list states.

- `01_IMG_2624_optimization_stacks_collapsed.PNG` - Stack cards with compact/collapsed medication rows.
- `02_IMG_2625_optimization_stacks_expanded.PNG` - Stack cards expanded to show medication details.

### 04_edit_protocol_flow

Edit protocol wizard, ordered by step.

- `01_IMG_2626_name_select_medications.PNG` - Step 1: name and medication selection.
- `02_IMG_2627_configure_dose.PNG` - Step 2: dose configuration.
- `03_IMG_2628_configure_schedule.PNG` - Step 2: schedule configuration.
- `04_IMG_2629_configure_preferences.PNG` - Step 2: preferences configuration.
- `05_IMG_2630_inventory_tracking_options.PNG` - Step 3: inventory and cost tracking toggles.
- `06_IMG_2631_review_and_save.PNG` - Step 4: review and save.

### 05_customize_home

Customize home screen/card library.

- `01_IMG_2632_customize_pinned_cards.PNG` - Pinned and next dose/compliance cards.
- `02_IMG_2633_customize_available_metrics.PNG` - Available metric cards, dose and benefits.
- `03_IMG_2634_customize_site_rotation_labs.PNG` - Site rotation, health, inventory, labs.
- `04_IMG_2635_customize_protocol_cycle_weight.PNG` - Protocol, cycle, weight, log cards.
- `05_IMG_2636_customize_health_metrics.PNG` - Body composition, sleep, resting HR, blood pressure.
- `06_IMG_2637_customize_tools_and_biomarkers.PNG` - Tools, biomarkers, recon, and data/simulation cards.

### 06_calendar_and_shots

Schedule views.

- `01_IMG_2638_calendar_month_with_due_shots.PNG` - Month calendar with selected day and upcoming shot preview.
- `02_IMG_2639_upcoming_shots_list.PNG` - Upcoming shots list.
- `03_IMG_2648_calendar_selected_day_add_dose.PNG` - Calendar selected day with scheduled doses and add dose action.

### 07_notifications

Notifications tabs.

- `01_IMG_2640_notifications_today_empty.PNG` - Today tab with empty/current state.
- `02_IMG_2641_notifications_upcoming.PNG` - Upcoming tab with scheduled medication notifications.
- `03_IMG_2642_notifications_reminders.PNG` - Reminders tab with health check reminder.

### 08_settings

Settings list, split by scroll position.

- `01_IMG_2643_settings_top.PNG` - Settings top: account, notifications, health, preferences, time zone.
- `02_IMG_2644_settings_bottom.PNG` - Settings bottom: data/privacy, support, app id/version.

### 09_dose_actions_and_logging

Dose actions and dose logging overlays.

- `01_IMG_2650_medication_pause_action_sheet.PNG` - Medication action sheet for pausing reminders temporarily.
- `02_IMG_2651_log_new_dose_basic_sheet.PNG` - Log new dose bottom sheet with dose, method, site, and notes.
- `03_IMG_2652_log_new_dose_advanced_sheet.PNG` - Expanded log new dose sheet with advanced fields like pain and site reaction.
